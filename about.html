This web application lets you search for music artists around the world. It also provides the users with a few famous albums of the artists. Search for an artist and the select an artist to look at his few of his famous albums. To get to know more information about the artist click More Info under the artist details.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap
